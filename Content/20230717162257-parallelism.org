#+title: Parallelism
#+filetags: :programming:

Parallelism implies the simultaneous execution of multiple independent processes.

[[id:618d0535-411d-4c36-b176-84413ec8bfc1][Concurrency]] describes the composition of independently executing processes : not saying anything regarding when these will be executed.

Concurrency is not parallelism.

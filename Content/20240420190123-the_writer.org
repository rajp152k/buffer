#+title: The Writer
#+filetags: :archetype:meta:

For an intellectual athelete, writing is just as important as the act of reading.

Balancing external sources and internal insights is the key to generating content worth consuming: being just the right amount of alienating and familiar so as to not sound too delusional and obvious.

When starting out and understanding the nature of content can be conveyed, invoking heavily upon [[id:20240420T190107.545784][The Reader]] becomes a necessity.

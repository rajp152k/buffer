#+title: Improvisation
#+filetags: :skills:meta:

Being prepared is the most under-rated hack out there.

Irrespective of how prepared you might be for any circumstance out there, preparing for being un-prepared is the next step in being prepared.

Improv is a skill that can explicitly be worked upon but once reasonably mastered, implicitly improves your life experiences without tedious overheads.

#+title: Entity
#+filetags: :meta:

Something that exists. Being.
Abstract, or tangible. 

An [[id:20240114T203847.960609][Entity can in turn have the potential to act]] (Agent) or [[id:20240114T203953.456605][react to actions by other able entities]] (Environment).


A collection of Agents and Environments that [[id:20240114T204304.033004][interact]] within themeselves can then be termed as a [[id:11847f5f-5337-425b-bacb-575f77690a4b][System]].

#+title: Interactions
#+filetags: :meta:

Whatever ensues when multiple [[id:20240114T203601.390070][Entities]] ([[id:20240114T203847.960609][Agent]]s or parts of the [[id:20240114T203953.456605][Environment]]) behave differently than if they were isolated may be termed as an interaction between the entities.

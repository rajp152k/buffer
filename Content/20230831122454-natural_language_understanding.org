#+title: Natural Language Understanding
#+filetags: :nlu:ai:

NLU aims to fill in the gap between natural and machine language. This involves learning language syntax, context, patterns, definitions, sentiment and intent.
Note that the focus of [[id:20230713T150554.400026][NLP]] is to represent natural language in a machine readable format.

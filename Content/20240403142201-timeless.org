#+title: timeless
#+filetags: :meta:

A notion with an eternally stabilized prominence in society since its conception and execution.

For instance, what would it take for one to produce a timeless piece of written excerpt?

What exact set of characteristics does it take to produce something that is timeless and eternally in intellectual demand - something truly fundamental, that would make you wonder what the world was doing before the realization of that notion.
